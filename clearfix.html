Clearfix

Summary

TO fix a float collapse we use a clear fix. The clear fix hack is used to prevent strange layout and cross-browser problems. To execute a clear fix you must clear the float after the floated elements in the container but before the close of the container. The technique needed to clear fix in our Rainforest site is called the easy clearing method. THis method employs a CSS pseudo selector to clear floats. This applies to a small bit of content, hidden from view, after the parent element. This clears the float.
